Praywake Privacy Policy

Effective July 12, 2026

We collect no data. None.
The short version: Praywake has no servers, no accounts, and no analytics. Your camera is used only on your device to detect that you are kneeling — no photo or video is ever recorded, stored, or sent anywhere. Your prayer history never leaves your phone.

Camera

Praywake uses your front camera during an alarm session to detect your body pose and confirm you are kneeling in prayer. This analysis happens entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework. Camera frames are processed in memory and immediately discarded — nothing is recorded, saved, uploaded, or shared. Praywake contains no code capable of transmitting images off your device.

Prayer history

Your completed prayers, streaks, and settings are stored only in the app's private storage on your phone. Deleting the app deletes them. We cannot see them — we have no server to send them to.

Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are processed by Apple through your Apple Account. We never see your payment details. Apple's handling of that data is described in the Apple Privacy Policy.

Notifications & alarms

Alarms are scheduled locally on your device using Apple's alarm and notification systems. No information about your alarms leaves your phone.

Your music

If you choose a song from your music library as your alarm, Praywake only reads the song you pick, only to play it. Your library is never scanned, indexed, or transmitted.

Analytics & tracking

Praywake contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and no trackers of any kind.

Children

Praywake does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children — because it does not collect information at all.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date. Because the app collects no data, we expect changes to be rare.
