Common questions

The alarm doesn't detect my kneeling.
Place your phone upright a couple of meters away, roughly hip height, so your shoulders and knees are both visible to the front camera. Kneeling toward the camera works — your ankles don't need to be visible.
Will the alarm ring if my phone is on silent?
Yes. Praywake uses the system alarm engine (iOS 26+), the same one the Clock app uses — it rings at full volume through silent mode and Focus.
Is the camera recording me?
No. Pose detection runs entirely on your device; no photo or video is ever recorded or uploaded. See our privacy policy — Praywake collects no data.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple: Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Praywake Plus. Lifetime purchases never renew.
Can I use my own music?
Praywake Plus lets you pick a song you own from your music library. Apple Music streaming songs are copy-protected by Apple and can't be used as alarm audio.
